Sack Trick Trivia and other Useless Facts

Robin's famous stipey hat was made by his brother

The Darkness played before Sack Trick at the Sam Powell memorial show on 26/2/02, at HQ's, London

The "Penguins On The Moon" cover is used as the link to the mailorder section on the ORG Records website

The first time Sack Trick played live was 12th May 1998 (6 days before my 10th birthday) at HQ's, London. The lineup was Chris Dale, Jef Streatfield, Robin Guy and Jem Davis.
